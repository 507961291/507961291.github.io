Welcome

If you are looking to open your business in our beach town, we invite you to JOIN NOW! We are here to establish a forum for business owners to collaborate and inform one another on events and initiatives that drive business. Individually, we can all do business, but together we can MAXIMIZE IT!

Our goal is to see a 15% year over year increase in revenue for our business owners. Our beach town has plenty of established businesses and have helped new shops open and integrate successfully.
